Both are [support by Bencher](/docs/explanation/adapters/).
So why choose Google Benchmark?
Google Benchmark integrates seamlessly with GoogleTest,
which is the de facto standard unit test harness in the C++ ecosystem.
I would suggest using Google Benchmark for benchmarking your code's latency,
especially if you are already using GoogleTest.
That is, Google Benchmark is great for measuring wall clock time.
