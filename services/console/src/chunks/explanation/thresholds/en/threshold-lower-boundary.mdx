## `--threshold-lower-boundary <BOUNDARY>`

<br />

Specify the Lower Boundary.
The constraints on the Lower Boundary depend on the [Test][tests] used.
Either the Lower Boundary, Upper Boundary, or both must be specified.

For details, see the documentation for the specific Test you are using:

<ul>
  <li>[Static Lower Boundary](#static-lower-boundary)</li>
  <li>[Percentage Lower Boundary](#percentage-lower-boundary)</li>
  <li>[z-score Lower Boundary](#z-score-lower-boundary)</li>
  <li>[t-test Lower Boundary](#t-test-lower-boundary)</li>
  <li>[Log Normal Lower Boundary](#log-normal-lower-boundary)</li>
  <li>[Interquartile Range Lower Boundary](#interquartile-range-lower-boundary)</li>
  <li>[Delta Interquartile Range Lower Boundary](#delta-interquartile-range-lower-boundary)</li>
</ul>

[tests]: #--threshold-test-test
